Hostel

The Jayshree Periwal International School is a place we love to come to and the icing on the cake is when we get to stay here. The residential program at Jayshree Periwal Group of Schools is an experience in itself and is a true sense of a home away from home. The environment is protective and attentive like home and, at the same time, makes children independent enough to grow as individuals in their own right. Our Boarding house has students from different nationalities and cultural backgrounds exposing them to a variety of cultures. The mission is not only to impart education in the classroom but also to help children become well-informed individuals.
